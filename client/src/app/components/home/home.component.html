About Us
VIS lab at Soongsil University is a human-computer interaction lab that aims to improve visual analytics with novel visualization and interaction techniques.
We embrace machine learning approaches in designing and developing visualization systems.
We are actively collaborating with a number of research groups to find remedies for practical problems.
